# Product Roadmap

## Current MVP

### Product Capabilities
- Supports four document types: Resume, Contract, Tender, Requirements.
- Provides structured decision output with dashboard-oriented slots.
- Includes baseline quality checks and document-type routing.

### Technical Milestones
- Stable analysis pipeline and normalized response model.
- Deterministic regression harness for core behavior validation.
- Baseline localization and slot consistency controls.

### Business Milestones
- Validate core value proposition in real user review loops.
- Establish trust in first-pass decision acceleration.
- Define measurable outcomes for time saved and decision consistency.

## Beta

### Product Capabilities
- Decision Framework-driven outputs aligned to role-specific goals.
- Stronger missing-information intelligence and confidence framing.
- Improved executive briefing and recommendation specificity by document type.

### Technical Milestones
- Expert Pack abstraction formalized as product contract.
- Expanded fixture corpus with edge-case scenarios per document type.
- Export structures aligned to operational handoff use cases.

### Business Milestones
- Pilot with cross-functional teams (HR, procurement, BA/Product).
- Validate reduction in review time and escalation churn.
- Capture category-level ROI narratives for go-to-market proof.

## V1

### Product Capabilities
- Production-grade Decision Intelligence experience for the four core types.
- Standardized recommendation quality with evidence and action linkage.
- Configurable dashboards and exports for organizational workflows.

### Technical Milestones
- Mature Expert Pack lifecycle (versioning, governance, validation).
- Reliability targets for latency, quality consistency, and traceability.
- Comprehensive evaluation suite for decision quality metrics.

### Business Milestones
- Convert pilots into repeatable customer deployments.
- Establish InsightFlow as decision-acceleration layer, not summarization tool.
- Define packaging aligned to team and workflow scale.

## V2

### Product Capabilities
- Multi-document decision orchestration across process stages.
- Organization-specific Expert Pack variants and policy overlays.
- Enhanced auditability and decision lineage for enterprise use.

### Technical Milestones
- Modular Expert Pack ecosystem with extensibility tooling.
- Decision graph support across related documents and events.
- Advanced governance controls for enterprise compliance requirements.

### Business Milestones
- Expand from single-team adoption to enterprise-wide operating model.
- Position InsightFlow as a category-defining Decision Intelligence platform.
- Build partner/ecosystem pathways around domain Expert Packs.
