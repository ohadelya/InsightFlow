# Value Proposition

## Resume
User: HR recruiter / hiring manager.
Pain: High candidate volume, low signal quality, inconsistent screening standards.
Existing workflow: Read CV manually, scan for clues, make subjective go/no-go calls.
How InsightFlow changes workflow: Surfaces decision-relevant evidence, highlights missing information, and proposes interview-focused next actions.
Time saved: Reduces first-pass review from full reading to decision-oriented inspection.
Decision improved: Improves consistency in continuation decisions and reduces false positives from vague profiles.

## Contract
User: Business owner / procurement lead / legal ops reviewer.
Pain: Clause-level risk is hard to detect quickly; key exposures are often missed.
Existing workflow: Manual clause scanning, ad hoc legal escalation, delayed alignment.
How InsightFlow changes workflow: Prioritizes exposure areas, identifies critical omissions, and frames negotiation-ready action points.
Time saved: Speeds pre-signing risk triage and reduces unnecessary full-document rereads.
Decision improved: Increases confidence in proceed/renegotiate/escalate decisions.

## Tender / RFP
User: Bid manager / delivery manager / commercial lead.
Pain: Bid/no-bid decisions are delayed by fragmented requirements and hidden compliance risks.
Existing workflow: Manual extraction of deadlines, requirements, and attachments with spreadsheet-based tracking.
How InsightFlow changes workflow: Consolidates feasibility, compliance-critical gaps, and submission-risk signals into one decision view.
Time saved: Accelerates bid qualification and readiness assessment.
Decision improved: Reduces avoidable no-bid mistakes and late disqualifications.

## Requirements
User: Business analyst / product manager / engineering lead.
Pain: Teams start delivery with unresolved ambiguity, causing rework and estimation drift.
Existing workflow: Read spec, collect scattered questions, clarify late during implementation.
How InsightFlow changes workflow: Exposes ambiguity and contradiction clusters, ranks clarification priorities, and maps to decision readiness.
Time saved: Shortens discovery-to-planning transition by front-loading critical clarifications.
Decision improved: Improves kickoff readiness and reduces downstream uncertainty.

## How InsightFlow Differs

### Versus Generic AI Chat
- Generic chat returns plausible text; InsightFlow returns decision-oriented outputs with role-specific framing.
- Generic chat often mixes analysis and speculation; InsightFlow emphasizes evidence binding and missing-information signaling.
- Generic chat is session-based; InsightFlow is workflow-integrated and export-oriented.

### Versus PDF Summarizers
- Summarizers compress content; InsightFlow prioritizes decision posture, risk, and next actions.
- Summarizers treat all content equally; InsightFlow ranks insights by business value.
- Summarizers rarely model missing critical information; InsightFlow treats missing data as first-class signal.

### Versus Notebook-Based Document Tools
- Notebooks are flexible but analyst-dependent and inconsistent across users.
- InsightFlow provides standardized expert reasoning patterns for repeatable decisions.
- Notebooks optimize exploration; InsightFlow optimizes operational decision speed and consistency.
