# Product Vision

## Why InsightFlow Exists
InsightFlow exists because high-stakes document decisions are often made under time pressure, fragmented context, and inconsistent analysis quality. Teams spend too much time reading documents and too little time making confident decisions.

## Customer Problem It Solves
Knowledge workers must review resumes, contracts, tenders, and requirements quickly, but current tools force manual synthesis. This creates three systemic issues:
- Slow decisions due to heavy reading burden.
- Inconsistent judgment across reviewers.
- Hidden risk because missing information is not surfaced early.

## Why Document Summarization Is Not Enough
Summarization compresses text, but decision-making requires interpretation. A short summary can still miss risk, omit critical gaps, or fail to answer the decision question. Users do not need shorter text; they need decision-ready intelligence anchored in evidence.

## What Decision Intelligence Means
Decision Intelligence in InsightFlow means transforming a document into:
- A clear decision posture.
- Evidence-backed insights ranked by business value.
- Explicit missing-information signals.
- Actionable next steps tied to uncertainty and risk.

It is not “what the document says.” It is “what the user should decide next, and why.”

## Product Mission
Enable professionals to make faster, higher-confidence document decisions by turning raw documents into expert-level, evidence-bound decision outputs.

## Long-Term Vision (2–3 Years)
- InsightFlow becomes a configurable Decision Intelligence platform where Expert Packs define domain logic, not hardcoded flows.
- Teams standardize decision quality across departments (HR, procurement, legal operations, product/engineering).
- Every decision output is traceable, auditable, and export-ready for operational workflows.
- InsightFlow evolves from single-document analysis to decision orchestration across document sets and process stages.
